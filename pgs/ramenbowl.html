Our Menu

Classic Ramen

Rich, savory broth with noodles and pork.

Ingredients: Broth: Pork bones, vegetables, and spices simmered for hours. Noodles: Fresh ramen noodles, chewy and flavorful. Pork Belly: Slow-braised, tender pork slices. Toppings: Soft-boiled egg, scallions, bamboo shoots, nori.

Steps:

Make Broth: Simmer pork bones, vegetables, and spices for 4–6 hours. Cook Noodles: Boil noodles and rinse with cold water. Braise Pork: Cook pork belly in soy sauce and mirin for 1–2 hours, then slice. Assemble: Place noodles in a bowl, pour broth, and add pork and toppings. Serve: Enjoy your ramen with optional chili flakes or sesame seeds.

Shoyu Ramen

Soy sauce-based broth with tender chicken and veggies.

Ingredients: Broth: Soy sauce, chicken stock, and spices. Noodles: Fresh ramen noodles. Chicken: Tender chicken, braised or grilled. Veggies: Scallions, mushrooms, and bok choy.

Steps:

Prepare Broth: Simmer soy sauce, chicken stock, and spices. Cook Noodles: Boil noodles and drain. Cook Chicken: Grill or braise chicken, then slice. Assemble: Combine noodles, broth, chicken, and veggies. Serve: Enjoy your Shoyu Ramen with optional toppings like bamboo shoots or soft-boiled eggs.

Spicy Miso Ramen

Spicy miso broth with chili and tender pork belly.

Ingredients: Broth: Miso paste, chili paste, and chicken stock. Noodles: Fresh ramen noodles. Pork Belly: Braised or grilled tender pork belly. Toppings: Chili flakes, scallions, and soft-boiled egg.

Steps:

Prepare Broth: Simmer miso paste, chili paste, and chicken stock for a spicy, savory base. Cook Noodles: Boil noodles and drain. Cook Pork Belly: Braise or grill pork belly, then slice. Assemble: Combine noodles, broth, pork belly, and toppings. Serve: Enjoy your Spicy Miso Ramen with extra chili flakes if desired.
